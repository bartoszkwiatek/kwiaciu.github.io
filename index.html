About me

My name is Bartosz Kwiatek. I graduated Wroclaw University Of Technology and Science as a Mechanical Engineer but decided to pursue career as Front End Developer.

History of my relationship with creating websites dates back to middle school when I took second place on school HTML competition. Another encounter was during university, when I discovered Python and framework Django - that's when I decided that being able to do both backend and frontend would be nice, but frontend is definitely more fun for me.

After graduating university I was working as a mechanical engineer, but inside my heart I felt that it was not what I was supposed to do. One day I found out about Google Developer Challenge Scholarship, and applied for Front End Developer course. But one may ask, if I started doing something to change my path, why it took so much time? And that would be reasonable doubt to express.

The answer to that is connected to my motivation in work. Despite the fact that things I did at work wasn't very meaningful to me, and I was not very excited by CAD. What I valued at work was great working atmosphere, nice people and good teammates, also I could make use of my problem solving skils. And that's what was enough to keep me from leaving, I wasn't perfectly happy but it was good enough.

However right now I took a step forward towards uncertain future. I am creating apps and websites and learing to sharpen my skills as Front End Developer and at the same time I am looking for career opportunities to gain professional experience.
